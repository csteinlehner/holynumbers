About

Holy numbers is a side project by Christoph Steinlehner. I'm an interaction & information designer usually based in Berlin, Germany. But currently I spend a lot of time in Jerusalem and travel through Israel and the Palestinian Territories.

Living here in the Holy Land and having uncommon cultural, religious and every day experiences inspired me to question my personal views with statistical data. Always having my german nurtured mind as background I want to challenge some prejudices with numbers.

Being a designer doing data visualizations is my natural approach to this endeavor. Of course I also write a few words about every visualization. But I'm not a journalist nor a scientist, so take everything you see here with a grain of salt.

I'm always open for feedback, ideas, etc. Get in touch via e-mail or hit me up on Twitter.
